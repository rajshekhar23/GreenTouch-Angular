About Us

We are an ISO 9001:2000 certified organization and hence quality is the prime objective of our organization. Due to this, we always strive hard to achieve zero defect production of Paints and Coatings. The raw material used by us is Titanium Dioxide, Minerals, Solvents, Alkyd-epoxy-polyurethane Resins And Oils. Our quality controllers stringently inspect each and every stage of the production starting from the sourcing of the raw material to the final dispatch of the products.
We use following well defined parameters in ensuring the best range of the Paints and Coatings:
Durability
Luster
Finishing
Defect free packaging
We continuously upgrade our technology to enhance the quality of our product and thereby attain maximum client satisfaction.
